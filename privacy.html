Privacy

Privacy for This Eisenhower Matrix Tool

Simple and specific: this tool stores task data in your browser and does not require an account.

Summary

  • Tasks are stored locally in your browser (localStorage).
  • No account is required.
  • No server-side task storage is used.

What’s stored locally

  • Your tasks and which quadrant each task is in.
  • Task metadata used by the app, such as task IDs and timestamps.
  • Your last selected quadrant when adding tasks.

Clearing this site’s data in your browser removes saved tasks.

What we don’t collect

  • No personal information collection by default.
  • No task content sent to a server by default.
  • No ads, no trackers, and no analytics scripts.

Third-party services

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts via fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com.

No other third-party services are used.

Your controls

  • Use the “Clear all tasks” button in the tool to remove current tasks.
  • Clear site storage in your browser settings to remove locally stored data.
  • In private browsing modes, local storage may be limited or cleared automatically.
