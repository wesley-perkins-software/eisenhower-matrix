About

About This Eisenhower Matrix Tool

A simple, local-first planner to help you choose what to do now, what to schedule, and what to drop.

At a glance

  • No accounts
  • Tasks saved locally on this device (localStorage)
  • No tracking
  • Works best on modern browsers

What this site is

This is a static Eisenhower Matrix tool for quickly sorting tasks by urgency and importance.

Why it exists

Many task apps are powerful but heavy. This tool is built for quick prioritization with minimal setup and fewer distractions.

Who it is for

Anyone managing mixed personal and professional tasks who wants a clearer daily plan without extra app overhead.

How it works

  • Add a task in the quick input.
  • Sort it into the best-fit quadrant.
  • Move tasks as priorities change.
  • Review your Schedule list regularly.

What it doesn’t do

  • No sync across devices
  • No collaboration
  • No reminders or calendar integration

Privacy summary

Your tasks stay in your browser on this device by default. Read full details on the Privacy page.
