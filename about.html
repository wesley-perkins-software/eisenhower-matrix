About

About This Eisenhower Matrix Tool

A lightweight matrix for people who want clarity without extra apps, accounts, or setup.

What this site is

This is a simple, static Eisenhower Matrix tool that helps you sort tasks by urgency and importance.

Why it exists

Most to-do tools add friction when you just need a fast decision. This one keeps the process short: add, sort, move, and focus.

Who it is for

Anyone juggling mixed-life tasks: home, school, health, work, and admin. No productivity jargon required.

Privacy-first by default

No login and no account. Tasks are stored in your browser only using localStorage.
