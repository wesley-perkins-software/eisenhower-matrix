# Privacy

This site does not require an account and does not store your tasks on a server.

## What is stored
Your tasks are saved locally in your browser (using localStorage) so they can persist when you return to the site on the same device and browser.

## What is not stored
- No server-side storage
- No accounts or logins
- No task data is sent to us

## Clearing your data
Use the “Clear all” button in the app, or clear this site’s storage in your browser settings.
